Recommended build order if you want to actually ship these (each delivers a new live signal in under a day of work):
  1. ApeWisdom → WSB momentum strategy — drop-in free, <2h. Highest expected info gain per hour.
  2. FINRA short volume → off-exchange short squeeze strategy — free CSV, <2h to wire, signal is identical to what Quiver sells.
  3. Finnhub free tier OR scrape OpenInsider → insider cluster buys — free or near-free, ~half-day. Cheaper-to-ship variant of the deferred Tier 3 SEC EDGAR build.
  4. pytrends → Google Trends momentum — free, <2h, watch for rate-limit flakiness in production.
  5. USPTO PatentsView → patent grants momentum — free but 1–2 days (need assignee→ticker mapping table).
  6. Apptopia or Sensor Tower → app downloads strategy — only justified after items 1–5 are paying off; ~$1k+/mo minimum.
