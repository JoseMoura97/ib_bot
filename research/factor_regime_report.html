Track 2 — Factor Regime ML

Methodology:
  • Expanding walk-forward CV · 240m min train · 6m embargo · 24m test steps
  • LightGBM: 5-seed ensemble (mean prob) to reduce stochastic noise (Fractus M8/M9 lesson)

  • Shuffle-label null test: expected AUC ≈ 0.50; actual null confirms no data leakage
  • Decade AUC: checks for distribution shift across time
  • Success bar: WF AUC ≥ 0.60, consistent across decades, above null
